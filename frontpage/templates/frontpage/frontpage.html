Who are we

Afro-wealth Investment consult is an independent consultancy firm and a research based think tank with experience in Economic Analysis, Regional and Market Analysis in various fields. Staffed with experienced and qualified experts from diverse background, we seek to deliver for our clients and stakeholders value for money and efficiency at the highest level. Afro-wealth Investment Consult offers up to date information on macroeconomic policy analysis, investment trends, security issues and other important services like Value Chain Investment analysis at their client’s request. We also engage in investor advice and analysis of the African market and other emerging economies to get the right and timely needed information for investment decisions.
